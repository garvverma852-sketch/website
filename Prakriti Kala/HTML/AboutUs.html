About Prakriti Kala

We are dedicated to empowering girls through the art of creativity. Our programs give them the skills, confidence, and community they need to flourish.

Our Mission

Creative Workshops

Hands-on art sessions where girls explore painting, embroidery, tie-dye, and more.

Collaborative Projects

We encourage teamwork through group projects that strengthen community bonds.

Exhibitions

Girls get the chance to showcase their creations and gain recognition for their talents.

Empowering Girls Through Creativity

Boosting Confidence

Our girls gain self-esteem, leadership skills, and pride in their creations through hands-on art activities.

Fostering Collaboration

Team-based projects allow girls to work together, build friendships, and strengthen community bonds.

Developing Skills

Girls learn practical and creative skills, from painting and embroidery to leadership and problem-solving.
